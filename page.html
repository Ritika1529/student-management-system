About

The Education Factory was established in 2005 and has a total of 20 classrooms.
The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
They offer education from KG to nursery.
The school has facilities such as a playground, library, canteen, gym,
computer lab, and others. The Education Factory has a Science Lab,

Admission Procedure

CBSE
THe EDUCATION FACTORY

To get admission to THe EDUCATION FACTORY,
students will be required to first register themselves. To register, parents and students will need to get
the offline application form from the school along with a prospectus.
Admission to the THe EDUCATION FACTORY is based
on the entrance exam conducted by the school. Admission
totally depends on the availability of seats. Also, the Principal has the
authority to cancel and admit students if they find fit.
After registration, the school will call students for an entrance
exam. After that, if students clear the exam, the fee payment
and further process will be done. Finally, admission will be
confirmed if the fee is paid and all required documents are submitted.
